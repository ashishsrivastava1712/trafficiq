7. Why XGBoost Won
XGBoost vs Other Models — Key Advantages
⚡
vs Linear
Non-linear patterns — traffic doesn't follow a straight line. XGBoost captures complex hour×weather interactions.
🌳
vs Decision Tree
300 trees vs 1 tree. Each tree corrects the previous one's mistakes → much lower MAE.
🧠
vs LSTM/DL
Tabular data with engineered features — DL needs raw sequences. XGBoost handles tabular data natively.
🎯
Regularization
Built-in L1/L2 regularization prevents overfitting. Only 0.027 train-test gap.
